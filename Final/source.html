Website Information Link
cults3d Cults3D is a online data repository that stores hundreds of gigabytes worth of STL Files, the website contains uploads from thousands of creators with content varying from niche parts to miniatures or statuettes. This is one of the more reliable websites that is unlikely to contain viruses, however nothing is foolproof, and as such it is reccomended that users have antivirus software. cults3d.com
Patreon Patreon is a website that allows designers and content creators to create, upload, and utilize media of multiple types. For the purposes of 3D-Printing Patreon offers monthly files that range from tabletop pieces to full statuettes or larger. Patreon.com
MyMiniFactory MyMiniFactory is a website much like cults in that it contains and sells a variety of 3d files, these are all distributed by independent modelers, however the website also hosts files designed by modern boardgame creators and their pieces. myminifactory.com
