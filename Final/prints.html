Services Offered

Here are a few models that I have printed over the course of the past 4 years, the idea of this section is to demonstrate the detail capable of being achieved at such a small scale. For the most part the models presented are no larger than a pen. These files were taken from across the websites listed on source, prepared, and printed by me for personal use in games or as shelf pieces.

The files presented belong to a various number of tabletop roleplaying games such as Dungeons & Dragons, as well as Warhammer 40,000. Due to how cost intensive first-party models are many in these communities design or print their own models taken from a variety of sources.
